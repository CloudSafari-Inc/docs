[Simbas.AI](http://Simbas.AI) is an AI-powered safari booking assistant that helps your team create, distribute, and sell trips at scale. By combining real-time chat, automated itinerary generation, and integrated payments, [Simbas.AI](http://Simbas.AI) eliminates operational bottlenecks and gives your team a genuine competitive edge.

<Note>
  [Simbas.AI](http://Simbas.AI) is built for tour operators, hotels and lodges, and travel agencies looking to streamline their operations.
</Note>


### **System Features**

#### **AI Chat for Real-Time Insights**

[Simbas.AI](http://Simbas.AI) gives your team access to an industry-specific conversational assistant — similar to ChatGPT, but purpose-built for safaris. The system enables your team to instantly ask questions about:

- Rates
- Availability
- Policies
- Special offers and discounts
- Any property or safari-related data

The chat is intelligently structured to guide your team through building complete itineraries in minutes.

#### **Key Benefits**

- **Instant access** to all property data — including rates, availability, policies, and more.
- **10x faster workflows** — get answers in seconds instead of hours.
- **Error reduction** — rates are automatically validated to prevent calculation mistakes.
- **Optimized pricing** — automatically applies discounts and special offers to unlock better rate

### **Static Itineraries Generated by AI**

From AI chat notes or directly through the dashboard, [Simbas.AI](http://Simbas.AI) automatically generates **editable, customer-ready itineraries** in a professional format — complete with accurate pricing and clean design.

#### **Key Benefits**

- **Create itineraries in minutes** instead of hours or days.
- **Deliver accurate, customer-friendly pricing** with zero manual effort.
- **AI Agents automatically verify details**, minimizing human error.
- **Pre-authorize payments instantly**, helping you secure revenue faster.

#### **How Does My Company Benefit?**

Simply put, [Simbas.AI](http://Simbas.AI) helps your team **build and distribute trips up to 100x faster**, freeing your time to focus on sales and customer relationships.
